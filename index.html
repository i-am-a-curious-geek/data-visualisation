# of Homicide Incidents by Date
The following depicts the geographical distributions of homicide crime rates between years 2010 - 2014. Based on the graph visualisations, it is evident that the most commonly used weapons in all homicide cases are firearms, which points to a possible overly lax reinforcement on firearms in the city of Baltimore.

Moreover, the Eastern and Northern districts of Baltimore has the greatest no. of homicide incidents in various time periods during years 2010 - 2014, which signals a potential lack of manpower at the Eastern and Northern police stations.

From the clustering of homicide incidents aggregated from years 2010 - 2014, both Eastern and North-Western police stations have the highest no. of homicide incidents occurring within its proximity relative to the other police stations in Baltimore. This signals that these 2 police stations are the ones which need the greatest allocation of manpower and law reinforcements as compared to other police stations of the city.
